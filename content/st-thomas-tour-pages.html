Frequently Asked Questions

St Thomas Tour FAQ

Common questions from cruise passengers planning their St Thomas shore excursion.

What is the best St Thomas shore excursion?
The best excursion depends on your travel style. Beach days suit relaxation seekers and families; snorkeling tours suit active travellers wanting a memorable underwater experience; and private tours suit families or groups wanting flexibility, custom timing and a more personalised island day.

Should I book a beach tour or a snorkeling tour?
Choose a beach tour for relaxation, simplicity and a stress free Caribbean day, ideal if you have younger children or prefer low intensity experiences. Choose a snorkeling tour for a more active, memorable Caribbean water experience with reef fish, coral and the chance to see marine life up close.

Are private tours worth it in St Thomas?
Private tours are often excellent value for families, couples and groups who want flexible timing and a more personalised experience. You get your own vehicle, your own guide, and the ability to spend more time at places you love and move on from those you don't, without waiting for a larger group.

What is the best excursion for first time visitors?
A sightseeing and beach combination is often the best all round choice for first time visitors. You get to see the island's iconic viewpoints and highlights, stop for shopping in Charlotte Amalie, and end with beach time, covering all the St Thomas highlights in one organised day.

How active are St Thomas snorkeling tours?
Snorkeling tours vary from moderate to active depending on the specific itinerary. Sailing and snorkeling tours are moderate, you swim comfortably at snorkel sites. Kayak and snorkel eco adventures require more physical effort. All snorkeling tours assume confidence in the water. Equipment is always provided.

Can I combine beaches and sightseeing on one tour?
Yes, several St Thomas tours combine scenic viewpoints, island sightseeing and beach stops in one excursion. The Shopping, Sightseeing & Beach Combo is designed exactly for this. Private tours also allow you to customise your own mix of viewpoints, beaches and stops.

Should I book St Thomas tours in advance?
Popular St Thomas tours can sell out during busy cruise periods, particularly private tours which have limited capacity. Advance booking is recommended to guarantee your preferred excursion. It also gives you time to plan your day properly and confirm all the logistics before your cruise.

What should I bring on a St Thomas shore excursion?
For any St Thomas excursion, bring: reef safe sunscreen, sunglasses, a hat, your ship card, US dollars in cash for tips and extras, comfortable footwear, and swimwear if your tour includes a water activity or beach stop. For snorkeling tours, you can bring your own mask and fins if you prefer, though equipment is always provided.
