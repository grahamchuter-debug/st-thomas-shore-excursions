Cruise Port Beaches

Best Beach Days
From the Cruise Port

St Thomas is known for calm bays, white sand and clear water. Beach excursions are among the most popular choices for cruise passengers who want a relaxed day ashore with easy transport and flexible return times.

Magens Bay is the island's most famous stretch of sand. Sapphire Beach and Lindquist Beach offer quieter alternatives on the east end. Most operators plan pickups near Havensight or Crown Bay.
