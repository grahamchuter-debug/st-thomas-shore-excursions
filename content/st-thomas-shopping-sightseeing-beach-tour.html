Day at a Glance

What To Expect on the Day

A typical St Thomas Shopping, Sightseeing & Beach excursion, from the cruise pier through sightseeing, shopping and beach time.

1

Meet and Depart

After meeting at the arranged point, typically at or near the cruise ship pier, the excursion gets underway. Confirm the exact meeting time and location with your operator before the day.

2

Island Sightseeing

The tour usually begins with scenic island driving, taking in classic St Thomas viewpoints and harbour panoramas. This is your introduction to the island, the views from the hillside roads are among the most photographed in the Caribbean.

3

Shopping Time

Shopping stops typically include the Charlotte Amalie area, where guests can browse duty free goods, jewellery, local crafts and waterfront retail. The order of sightseeing and shopping may vary depending on the route.

4

Beach Stop

A beach stop gives guests time to swim, sunbathe and enjoy the turquoise Caribbean water. Exact beach location and facilities vary by tour, confirm what is included and whether sun loungers or amenities are available.

5

Return to the Cruise Port

The tour returns to the cruise ship pier in time for your ship's schedule. Always know your all aboard time and build in a buffer, traffic near the port on busy cruise days can be unpredictable.

Note: The order of activities may vary depending on the day's route, traffic and timing. Always confirm the specific itinerary with your operator before booking.

Helpful Advice

Tips for Enjoying This Tour

Simple pointers to help you make the most of your sightseeing, shopping and beach day in St Thomas.

1
Wear comfortable clothing and shoes
You'll be walking at viewpoints, shopping areas and the beach. Comfortable, breathable clothing and footwear makes the day much easier.
2
Bring swimwear if you plan to enter the water
Wear swimwear under your clothing so you are ready for the beach stop without needing to change in limited facilities.
3
Pack reef safe sunscreen
The Caribbean sun is strong. Apply before you leave the ship and reapply at the beach stop, reef safe formulas protect both you and the marine environment.
4
Bring cash or card for shopping, food and extras
Shopping, refreshments, beach amenities and tips are typically not included. A mix of cash and card covers most situations in Charlotte Amalie and at the beach.
5
Keep valuables secure while at the beach
Avoid bringing expensive jewellery or unnecessary valuables to the beach. Use a waterproof pouch for your phone and keep essentials close.
6
Allow time to return before all aboard
Always know your ship's all aboard time. Build in a comfortable buffer, traffic near the cruise pier can be slow on busy port days with multiple ships in dock.
7
Travel light so shopping and beach time are easier
A small daypack or tote bag works best. The less you carry at the start of the day, the more comfortable shopping becomes, and the easier it is to enjoy the beach stop.

FAQs

St Thomas Shopping, Sightseeing & Beach Tour FAQ

Common questions from cruise passengers planning this classic St Thomas combo excursion.

Is this tour good for first time visitors?
Yes. This tour is well suited to first time visitors because it combines several classic St Thomas experiences in one excursion, island sightseeing, shopping and a beach stop, giving guests a well rounded introduction to the island without needing to plan separately.

Does the tour include beach time?
Yes. The tour includes a beach stop as part of the itinerary, although exact timing, location and facilities may vary by operator. Confirm which beach is included and what amenities are available before booking.

Is this a very active tour?
No. This is generally less active than snorkeling, kayaking or hiking tours. The activity level is easy, the tour primarily involves scenic driving, walking at viewpoints and beach relaxation rather than strenuous physical activity.

Can I shop during the tour?
Yes. Shopping opportunities are a core part of this excursion. St Thomas is one of the Caribbean's most popular duty free shopping destinations, and the tour typically includes time in or near Charlotte Amalie's shopping areas. Bring cash or card for purchases.

Is this tour suitable for families?
Yes. Families often like this tour because it includes variety without being too physically demanding. The easy activity level, beach stop and mix of sightseeing and shopping suits a range of ages. Always check specific age requirements and inclusions with your operator.

Should I bring swimwear?
Yes. Bring swimwear under your clothing if you plan to swim during the beach stop. Wearing it from the start of the day means you are ready without needing to change, which makes the beach stop easier and more enjoyable.

Does the tour return to the cruise port?
This type of shore excursion is designed for cruise passengers and should return to the cruise port in time for your ship's schedule. Always check the exact meeting point, return timing and drop off details with your operator before booking, and always know your all aboard time.

Is this better than a private tour?
It depends on what your group wants. Choose this tour if you want a simple, organised combination of sightseeing, shopping and beach time without extra planning. Choose a private St Thomas tour if you want more flexibility, a custom route and a vehicle reserved exclusively for your group.
