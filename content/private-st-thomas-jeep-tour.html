Day at a Glance

What to Expect on the Day

A typical Private St Thomas Jeep Adventure, from the cruise pier to the island's best viewpoints and back.

1

Meet Your Private Tour

After meeting at the agreed point, typically near the cruise ship pier, your private jeep style adventure begins. No waiting for a full coach, no strangers joining your group. Just your party, your guide and the road ahead.

2

Island Roads and Coastal Scenery

Your adventure heads out into the island's hillside roads, coastal curves and scenic inland routes. Depending on the route and conditions, you may pass through island neighbourhoods, along clifftop roads and through areas of classic Caribbean scenery.

3

Viewpoint Stops and Photo Opportunities

Your tour stops at scenic viewpoints where guests can take in panoramic harbour views, hillside scenery and Caribbean coastline. Possible stops may include areas such as Skyline Lookout or Drake's Seat, among the most photographed spots on the island.

4

Flexible Exploration

The private format means your guide can adapt to your group's interest and energy. Whether you want more time at a particular viewpoint, an extra photo stop or a scenic detour, the experience is shaped around your party rather than a fixed schedule.

5

Return to the Cruise Port

After your adventure, your private tour returns to the cruise ship pier with timing planned around your ship's schedule. You'll return with photographs, island memories and a very different story to tell at dinner than the guests who never left the beach.

Note: Exact routes, stops and timings vary by operator, weather and local conditions. Always confirm the specific itinerary with your operator at booking.

Expert Tips

Tips for Enjoying Your Private Jeep Tour

Make the most of your private St Thomas island adventure with these practical tips.

1
Bring sunglasses and sun protection
Open air touring in Caribbean sun means sun exposure from start to finish. Hat, sunglasses and sunscreen are all essential.
2
Wear comfortable light clothing
Lightweight, breathable clothes are ideal. You'll be in and out of the vehicle at viewpoints, so comfort matters more than formality.
3
Bring a camera or phone for viewpoints
The scenic stops on this tour are genuinely worth photographing. Make sure your device is charged and has storage space before you go.
4
Check meeting details carefully
Private tours vary in meeting point by operator. Confirm your exact pickup location and time well in advance of the day.
5
Confirm what is included before booking
Some tours include water or a guide narrative; others are more self directed. Knowing what's included avoids surprises on the day.
6
Bring cash or card for optional extras
You may want to stop for refreshments, purchase souvenirs at a viewpoint, or cover tips. A small amount of cash covers most scenarios.
7
Allow time to return before all aboard
Always know your ship's all aboard time and build in a comfortable buffer. Traffic near the cruise pier on busy port days can be unpredictable.
8
Consider a beach tour if you want swim time
This tour is focused on scenic sightseeing and island roads rather than beach time. If you want to swim, explore St Thomas beach excursions instead.

FAQs

Private St Thomas Jeep Tour FAQ

Common questions from cruise passengers planning this private island adventure.

Is this a private tour?
Yes. This tour is designed as a private St Thomas experience for your party. You are not sharing the vehicle or the experience with other groups. The adventure is arranged around your schedule and your preferences.

Is this suitable for cruise passengers?
Yes. This tour is designed with cruise passengers in mind. Always check the exact meeting point, duration and return time against your ship's schedule before booking. Private tours can be more flexible than group excursions, but your all aboard time is always the priority.

What kind of places might we see?
Possible highlights may include scenic viewpoints, coastal roads, harbour views and island photo stops such as Skyline Lookout or Drake's Seat, two of the most iconic vantage points in St Thomas. Exact stops depend on your operator and route. Confirm the highlights before booking.

Is this a beach tour?
This tour is focused on scenic island adventure and sightseeing rather than beach time. If you want a beach focused private experience, consider a private Sapphire Beach tour or browse the full range of St Thomas beach excursions for alternatives.

Is it suitable for families?
It can be a great choice for adventurous families, especially those with older children or teenagers who enjoy sightseeing, island roads and an energetic experience. Always check minimum age requirements and vehicle suitability with your operator before booking.

Is this tour very active?
It is more active in feel than a coach tour, but not as physically demanding as kayaking or hiking excursions. Most of the time is spent in the vehicle between scenic stops, with short periods of walking at viewpoints. Check the activity level details with your operator before booking.

What should I bring?
Bring sunglasses, reef safe sunscreen, a hat, comfortable light clothing, a camera or fully charged phone and cash or card for optional extras or tips. If your route includes a beach or swim stop, bring swimwear. Confirm this with your operator before the day.

Should I book in advance?
Yes. Private tours have limited availability compared to large group excursions, and popular St Thomas port days can book up quickly. Booking in advance secures your preferred time and avoids disappointment on the day.
