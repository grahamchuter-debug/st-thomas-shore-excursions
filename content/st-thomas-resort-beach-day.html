What to Expect on the Day

A typical resort beach day for St Thomas cruise passengers: relaxed, flexible, and entirely your own.

1

Meet at the Cruise Pier

Your guide or shuttle meets you at the Havensight or Crown Bay cruise terminal. A short drive (15 to 30 minutes depending on beach location) takes you to your resort beach.

2

Arrive and Settle In

Find your sun lounger, drop your beach bag, and take in the view. Changing rooms and restrooms are typically available. Order a drink from the beach bar if you like. The day is yours.

3

Swim, Relax, and Explore

Take a dip in the calm Caribbean water, float on an inflatable, or simply stretch out and read. If you want a bit more activity, ask about optional snorkeling gear, kayaks, or paddleboards available to rent on site.

4

Photo Opportunities

St Thomas resort beaches deliver postcard worthy views: soft white sand framed by palms, turquoise water, and green hillsides. Your camera (and your memory) will thank you.

5

Return to Your Ship

Transport returns you to the cruise terminal well before sail away time. You'll arrive back on board relaxed, sun kissed, and carrying the best version of that first day Caribbean feeling.

Insider Tips

6 Tips for the Perfect Beach Day

Make the most of every minute on the sand with these practical tips for cruise passengers.

🧴

Use Reef Safe Sunscreen

Many Caribbean beaches require reef safe sunscreen and it's the right thing to do anyway. Mineral based SPF 50+ is ideal for a full day in tropical sun.

💧

Stay Hydrated

The Caribbean sun is deceptively strong. Drink water regularly throughout the day. It's easy to forget when you're relaxing and having fun.

💳

Bring Small Cash

The beach bar and optional activity rentals are usually cash friendly. Bring small USD bills. US dollars are the currency of St Thomas and widely accepted.

🔒

Protect Your Valuables

A waterproof phone pouch and a small dry bag are worth every penny. Leave anything irreplaceable in your cabin safe. You won't need it on the beach.

⏰

Watch Your Return Time

Your excursion operator will give you a meeting time. Note it down and set a phone alarm. It's surprisingly easy to lose track of time when you're enjoying the Caribbean.

🤿

Try Snorkeling if Available

Even if you've never snorkeled before, St Thomas's calm, clear water makes it one of the best places to start. Ask about rental equipment. You might be pleasantly surprised.

FAQs

Beach Day Questions Answered

Common questions from cruise passengers planning a St Thomas resort beach day.

What is included in a St Thomas resort beach day excursion?
A typical resort beach day includes round trip transportation from the cruise pier to a resort beach, beach access, and use of sun loungers or chairs. Some packages include a welcome drink, snorkeling gear rental, or lunch. Check your specific booking for what's included, and what's available to add on at extra cost.

Which beach will I go to on a resort beach day?
Common options include Sapphire Beach, Magens Bay, and Secret Harbour Beach. Each has its own character. Sapphire is calm and sheltered, Magens Bay is wide and scenic, and Secret Harbour is quieter and more boutique. Your booking details will specify which beach is included.

Will I be back at the ship before sail away?
Yes. Shore excursion operators plan return transport so you're back at the pier well before your ship's sail away time. You'll receive a clear meeting time for the return shuttle. Set a phone alarm for it and you won't need to worry.

Is food and drink available at the resort beach?
Most resort beaches in St Thomas have an on site beach bar and/or restaurant serving food, cocktails, soft drinks, and snacks. These are typically not included in the excursion price (unless specified), so bring USD cash for extras. Prices are reasonable by resort standards.

Is this excursion suitable for young children?
Absolutely. A resort beach day is one of the most family friendly shore excursions in St Thomas. The beaches used are calm and sheltered, making them safe for paddling. Children typically love the setting, and parents get to actually relax, which is a bonus in itself.

Can I snorkel on a resort beach day?
In many cases, yes. Snorkeling gear rental is available at or near the beach. Sapphire Beach in particular has excellent snorkeling conditions with marine life visible close to shore. If snorkeling is a priority, check that your chosen beach has rental equipment available, or consider a dedicated St Thomas snorkeling tour for a more guided underwater experience.

What currency should I bring to St Thomas?
St Thomas is a US territory, so the currency is the US dollar. No exchange required. Cards are accepted at most resort beach facilities, but small cash is useful for tips, small purchases, or activity rentals. Bring a mix of bills ranging from $1 to $20.

How is a resort beach day different from going to the beach independently?
A resort beach day packages the transport, beach access, and amenities into one seamless experience. Going independently saves money but requires you to arrange your own taxi, navigate fare negotiations, and manage return timing yourself. For first timers or anyone who values convenience and ship safe return guarantees, the excursion format is usually worth it.
