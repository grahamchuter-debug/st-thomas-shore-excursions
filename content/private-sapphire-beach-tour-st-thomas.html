Day at a Glance

What to Expect on the Day

A typical Private Coastal Drive & Sapphire Beach Break, from cruise pier to beach and back.

1

Meet at the Cruise Ship Pier

Your private tour begins at the cruise ship pier. Your driver or guide meets your party and gets you settled into your private vehicle. No waiting for other passengers, no shared coach loading, just your group, ready to go.

2

Scenic Coastal Drive

Your private tour takes in scenic island roads, coastal views and, depending on the route, viewpoints overlooking the harbour and surrounding islands. A relaxed and scenic introduction to St Thomas before the beach.

3

Arrive at Sapphire Beach

After the scenic drive, you arrive at Sapphire Beach, one of the most visited and appreciated beach areas in St Thomas for cruise passengers. Known for turquoise water, island views and a relaxed Caribbean atmosphere.

4

Beach Time (around 2.5 hours)

Guests usually have around 2.5 hours at Sapphire Beach. Swim, relax in the sun, take photographs or arrange optional extras at the beach. Chairs, umbrellas, food, drinks and water activities may be available at additional cost.

5

Return to the Cruise Port

After beach time, your private tour returns to the cruise ship pier with timing planned around your ship's schedule. A comfortable, unhurried return with no shared coach logistics to navigate.

Note: Exact routes, timings and stops may vary by operator and conditions. Always confirm the specific itinerary with your operator at booking.

Expert Tips

Tips for Enjoying Your Private Beach Tour

Make the most of your private St Thomas experience with these practical tips from experienced shore excursion guests.

1
Wear swimwear under light clothing
You'll go from vehicle to beach, having swimwear ready saves time and avoids an awkward change.
2
Pack reef safe sunscreen
Required for marine protected areas and kinder to St Thomas' reefs. Apply before you leave the ship.
3
Bring cash or card for beach extras
Chairs, umbrellas, food, drinks and rentals may not be included. A small amount of cash or a card covers most options.
4
Confirm inclusions before booking
Inclusions can vary between operators. Always check what's covered so you're not surprised at the beach.
5
Allow time to return before all aboard
Always know your ship's all aboard time and build in a comfortable buffer for the return journey.
6
Travel light for easier beach time
Leave valuables on the ship where possible. A small beach bag with your essentials is all you need.
7
Bring sunglasses, a towel and comfortable sandals
Essential for both the coastal drive and the beach. Comfortable sandals work well for both elements of the day.

FAQs

Private Sapphire Beach Tour FAQ

Common questions from cruise passengers planning this private St Thomas shore excursion.

Is this tour private?
Yes. This is a private St Thomas shore excursion reserved for your party rather than a large shared group tour. You travel in a private vehicle with just your own group, giving you a more flexible and personal experience.

How long is the tour?
The tour is usually around 4 hours in total, including the scenic coastal drive and time at Sapphire Beach. This makes it a well paced half day excursion that fits most cruise port schedules comfortably.

Does the tour include Sapphire Beach?
Yes. The tour includes time at Sapphire Beach, usually around 2.5 hours depending on conditions and your operator's schedule. This gives you meaningful time to swim, relax and enjoy the beach, not just a brief stop.

Does it leave from the cruise port?
Yes. The tour usually leaves from the cruise ship pier, making it a convenient option for cruise passengers without any separate transfer logistics to arrange. Always confirm the exact meeting point with your operator before the day.

Are beach chairs and umbrellas included?
Beach chairs, umbrellas, snorkeling gear, food, drinks and water activities may cost extra and may not be included in the tour price. Always check current tour inclusions with your operator before booking so you know what to budget for on the day.

Is this tour wheelchair accessible?
No. This tour is generally not wheelchair or scooter accessible. Guests with mobility requirements should check alternative St Thomas shore excursion options before booking.

Is this good for families?
Yes. Families often choose this tour because it offers private transport, a scenic coastal drive and beach time in one straightforward itinerary. The moderate pacing and comfortable private vehicle make it accessible for most family groups. Always confirm any minimum age requirements with your operator before booking.

Is Sapphire Beach good for swimming?
Sapphire Beach is popular for swimming, scenery and light snorkeling, although conditions can vary by day. It is generally considered one of the better swimming beaches in St Thomas for cruise passengers, with turquoise water and a scenic Caribbean setting.
