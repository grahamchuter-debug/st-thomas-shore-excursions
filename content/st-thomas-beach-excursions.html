Why St Thomas Is One of the
Caribbean's Best Beach Cruise Ports

St Thomas is one of the most popular Caribbean cruise destinations for beach lovers. With calm turquoise water, scenic coastlines and a wide choice of beaches close to the cruise port, it is easy to enjoy a relaxing beach day during your time ashore.

Whether you want a luxury resort style beach experience, a scenic sightseeing tour with beach stops or simply time to relax in the Caribbean sun, St Thomas offers beach excursions for every type of cruise passenger.

Many visitors choose organised beach excursions because they simplify transport, timing and logistics, leaving more time to enjoy the island.

Common Questions

St Thomas Beach Excursions FAQ

What is the best beach excursion in St Thomas?
Many cruise passengers choose Magens Bay, Sapphire Beach or a resort beach day excursion depending on whether they want relaxation, snorkeling or sightseeing. The resort beach day excursion is ideal for those who want a comfortable, facilities included experience, while Magens Bay is perfect for those who simply want a beautiful Caribbean beach.

How far are the beaches from the St Thomas cruise port?
Many popular beaches are within approximately 15 to 30 minutes of the cruise port depending on traffic and location. Organised excursions take care of transport, making the journey simple.

Is Magens Bay worth visiting on a cruise stop?
Yes. Magens Bay is one of the Caribbean's most famous beaches and a very popular choice for cruise passengers. Its calm, shallow water, sheltered bay and scenic surroundings make it a strong choice for families, couples and first time visitors alike.

Are beach chairs included on St Thomas beach excursions?
Some organised beach excursions include beach chairs and resort facilities, particularly resort beach day experiences. Others may offer optional rentals at the beach. Check the specific inclusions for your chosen excursion before booking.

Can I combine sightseeing and beach time in St Thomas?
Yes. Several St Thomas excursions combine scenic island viewpoints, shopping in Charlotte Amalie and beach stops into one convenient experience. The Shopping, Sightseeing & Beach Combo tour is a popular choice for cruise passengers who want variety during their port day.

Are St Thomas beach excursions suitable for families?
Yes. Many beach excursions are family friendly and well suited to guests with children. The calm, shallow waters at Magens Bay in particular are excellent for younger swimmers. The resort beach day excursion also works well for families wanting a comfortable, organised day out.

What should I wear for a beach excursion in St Thomas?
Lightweight clothing, swimwear and comfortable sandals are recommended for most St Thomas beach tours. A light cover up is useful if you plan to stop at Charlotte Amalie or other sightseeing spots. Don't forget reef safe sunscreen and sunglasses for the Caribbean sun.
